Who We Are
Backpack Buddies of Maple Valley (WA) is a non-profit organization that
serves students in the Tahoma School District. Our vision is to help create a
community where nutritional needs of all Tahoma students are met and stu-
dents are able to thrive and be successful at home, at school and in extracurric-
ular activities.
Who We Serve
We serve students who face nutritional insecurity over the weekend be-
cause they are lacking sufficient nutrition at home or are unsure where their
next meal will come from. Students families enroll voluntarily and their identi-
ty is only known to the counselor at their school.
What We Do
Each week during the school year, volunteers assemble bags full of non -
perishable foods and fresh fruit. These bags are then delivered to students on
the last day of the week (typically Friday).

Food List
Our goal is to supplement food the student has at home. Knowing that liv-
ing conditions may vary, we provide nonperishable food items along with fresh
fruit. We also wish to provide items that are easily prepared. Backpack Buddies
runs on donations alone and look for sales and deals when purchasing our food.
Brands may change, but overall ingredients in the bag remain the same.
 macaroni and cheese
 canned soup or pasta
 chocolate milk
 juice
 popcorn
 applesauce or fruit cups
 instant oatmeal
 granola or snack bars
 snack crackers (cheese and peanut butter)
 pudding cup
 fresh fruit

Get Involved
We operate solely on a dedicated group of volunteers and we’d love to
have you join us! There are different ways to get involved.
During the school year we pack bags, nearly every week, on Thursday eve-
nings. Some volunteers take one week each month and others fill in, here and
there, when their schedule allows. Volunteers pick up and deliver food to our
headquarters twice a month and they deliver bags to each elementary and mid-
dle school each week. We also have various fundraising events that could not
happen without volunteers.
Looking for a place to get involved? Visit our Sign Up Genius site to see
what opportunities are currently available. Don’t see what you’re looking for?
Have an idea or a talent that could benefit Backpack Buddies? Contact us!
